Lesson 1: Welcome to the Nanodegree

This lesson introduces the student to the Nanodegree Program. One of the key takeaways from this lesson is how to Think Like a Programmer.
Thinking like a programmer consists of the following:

  • Procedural Thinking
  • Abstract Thinking
  • Systems Thinking
  • Technological Empathy
  • Debugging
